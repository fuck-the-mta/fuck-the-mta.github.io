Fuck the MTA

Fuck the MTA is a political action committee primarily dedicated to the goal of making New York City's official motto “Fuck the MTA.” The secondary goal of Fuck the MTA is to promote policies to improve the public transportation for all residents of New York City (even those in Staten Island) to the point at which "Fuck the MTA" is not the most common phrase uttered in the city.

We are currently awaiting approval from the FEC, after which we will open up donations, with which we will use for purchasing advertising space across the city to promote our stated goals.
